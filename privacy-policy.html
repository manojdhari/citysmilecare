Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At City Smile Care, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect your name, phone number, and email if you submit a form or contact us.
  • Usage Data: We collect non-personally identifiable data such as browser type, device, pages visited, and time spent on our website.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance site performance and gather analytics. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect you with licensed dental providers based on your inquiry or search.
  • To respond to your questions or support requests.
  • To analyze site traffic and improve our website performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who help operate our website or connect you with dental professionals. These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Your Choices

  • You may contact us to review, update, or delete your personal information.
  • You may disable cookies in your browser, though it may affect site performance.

6. External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

City Smile Care does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child has submitted personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the date above to reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of our website means you accept the revised policy.
