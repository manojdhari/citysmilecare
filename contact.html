Contact FAQ

How quickly will someone respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business hours during regular business hours. For urgent matters or emergency dental needs, we recommend calling us directly at (773) 915-6405 for immediate assistance.

Do I need to provide my location to get help?

Yes, providing your location (city, state, or ZIP code) helps us connect you with the most appropriate dental professionals in your area. This ensures you receive care from licensed providers near you.

Can I call after hours for emergency dental needs?

Our phone line at (773) 915-6405 is available 24/7 for emergency dental needs. We understand that dental emergencies don't follow business hours, and we're committed to helping you find appropriate care regardless of when you call.

Will City Smile Care schedule appointments for me?

City Smile Care is a dental connection service. We help connect you with appropriate dental professionals based on your needs and location. While we don't schedule appointments directly, we can provide you with contact information for suitable providers who can schedule your appointment.
