Frequently Asked Questions

How does City Smile Care work?

City Smile Care connects you with licensed dental professionals nationwide. Simply enter your location, and we'll help match you with verified dentists based on your needs and their availability.

What dental services can I find through City Smile Care?

Through City Smile Care, you can connect with dentists offering a wide range of services including regular cleanings, dental implants, emergency dentistry, root canal therapy, gum disease treatment, cosmetic dentistry, and pediatric dental care.

Is City Smile Care available in my area?

Yes, City Smile Care serves all 50 states across the USA. Our network includes licensed dental professionals nationwide, making it easy to find dental care no matter where you're located.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Appointment availability varies based on your location and the type of service needed. For emergency dental care, we can often connect you with a provider the same day. For routine care, appointments may be available within a few days to a week.

Are all dentists in your network licensed?

Yes, all dental professionals in our network are licensed and follow industry standards. We verify credentials to ensure you receive quality care from qualified providers.
