Our Story

Connecting People With Quality Dental Care

City Smile Care was founded with a simple mission: to make quality dental care accessible to everyone across the United States. We recognized that many people struggle to find reliable, licensed dental professionals when they need them most, especially in emergency situations or when relocating to a new area.
